Cofe Cafe

Address / 2F., No. 248, Sec. 1, Dihua St., Datong Dist., Taipei City
Open from 10:00 to 18:00.
Minimum charge is NTD $150/per person.

If you want to find a great quite place with delicious food and good tea, Cofe would be one of the best cafe in Taiwan you can go.Simple as its toast may be ,great flavor of which can't never be underestimated. Try it, you will know how tasty it is ,and it is definitely worth visiting.By the way, Cofe is also famous for the special chocolate and cafe. Don't forget try those, when you have time visiting there.

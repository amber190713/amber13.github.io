Mumi Cafe

Address / No. 1, Ln. 31, Rui'an St., Da’an Dist., Taipei City
Open from Tuesday to Sunday, from 11:00 to 19:00.
Reservation is not allowed.

Mumi Cafe is an Korean style cafe, which is famous for self-baked toast.The holder of the shop is a Korean, and the workers in the shop also speak Korean.As a result, you might think you are in Soul as you walked in the cafe.As for the desserts in the shop, they are definitely authentic Korean food and have delicious taste.
